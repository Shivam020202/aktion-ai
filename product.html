Meeting Transcript

Capture Every Word, Never Miss a Detail

AktionAI ensures that every meeting is accurately transcribed in real time, turning conversations into structured, searchable text. With AI-powered transcription, you can:

  • ✔️ Get real-time transcriptions for video and audio meetings with high accuracy.
  • ✔️ Search key discussions instantly without rewatching entire meetings.
  • ✔️ Store and organize transcripts for easy reference and knowledge sharing.
Task Categorization

Turn Discussions into Actionable Tasks Instantly

No need to manually sort through meeting notes—AktionAI automatically extracts key action items and organizes them into well-defined tasks. It enables you to:

  • ✔️ Identify and prioritize tasks based on urgency and relevance.
  • ✔️ Automatically group similar tasks under relevant categories.
  • ✔️ Ensure clarity by eliminating ambiguity in task descriptions and objectives.
AI Assigner

Smart Task Assignments for Efficient Teamwork

AktionAI doesn't just extract tasks—it intelligently assigns them to the right people, ensuring efficient collaboration and execution. With AI-powered task assignments, you can:

  • ✔️ Match tasks to the right team members based on role, expertise, and workload.
  • ✔️ Automate task delegation to save time and reduce manual effort.
  • ✔️ Ensure accountability with clear ownership and responsibility tracking.
